## [Update pt_BR translation and standardize new strings #1063](https://github.com/Jeffser/Alpaca/pull/1063)

This commit updates the Brazilian Portuguese (pt_BR) translation to include the latest strings from the repository and ensures full consistency with the established technical glossary.

The primary goal remains to maintain a professional, clear, and standardized user experience.

---

### Key Standardization Summary (New or Reinforced Terms)

This section outlines the most significant standardizations applied to new strings introduced since the last commit:

1. **Adaptations for New Features:**
    - `Port` (code/components) is consistently translated as **"Adaptar"** (e.g., "Adaptados para blp").
    - `Agentic` is translated as **"Agentividade"** or **"Agente"** (depending on context, referencing the AI's planning capability).
    - `Transcribe` is standardized to **"Transcrever"** and `Transcriber` to **"Transcritor"**.
    - `Image Viewer` is translated as **"Visualizador de Imagens"**.
    - `Retry` is standardized to **"Tentar Novamente"**.

2. **Technical Terms Retained (Anglicisms):**
    - The terms **`redesign`**, **`podcast`**, **`widget`**, **`benchmark`**, and **`vibe coding`** are retained as widely accepted anglicisms in their respective technical contexts.
    - **`Input`** is translated as **"Entrada"** when referring to data/text input.

3. **Consistency Maintenance:**
    - Confirmed the distinction between **`Port`** (network, translated as **"Porta"**) and **`Port`** (code, translated as **"Adaptar"**).
    - Maintained the use of **"Excluir," "Executar,"** and **"Gerar Novamente"** for all applicable new strings.
